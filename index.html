CoEP Technological University

  • Pune is called as the ‘Oxford of the East’, as it is a hub to many educational institutes. College of Engineering Pune, Technological University (COEP Tech) is a prominent educational institute in Pune as it is the 3rd oldest engineering college in Asia. Established in 1854, this institute is a 168-year-old legacy. COEP Tech has many notable alumni from different walks of life like Bharat Ratna Mr. M Visvesvaraya, Prof. Thomas Kailath, Dr. C. K. N. Patel and many more to name. COEP Tech strives for the overall development of its students. With outstanding curriculum, COEP Tech provides various co-curricular and extracurricular activities. With its strong history and foundation, COEP Tech continues to shape exceptional engineers, innovators, entrepreneurs and personalities from the students. With an aim to continue its unparalleled legacy, the COEP Tech workforce and its brilliant young minds continue to pursue their ambitions with zeal, courage and rigor.

CoEP's

BHAU Innovation & Entrepreneurship Cell

  • It is a students led club, supported by the institute and mentored by faculty. BHAU's Innovation and Entrepreneurship Cell, COEP Tech aims to foster a culture of entrepreneurship among students. The cell has been organizing various engaging events to inculcate an interest in entrepreneurship and start-ups. For example, IDEA competition, B-Plan Competition, Case Study Activity, Budgeting, Workshops, and seminars, etc. Pune Start-Up Fest (PSF) is the annual entrepreneurial festival organized by the I&E-Cell of COEP Tech. COEP Tech's I&E-Cell is a pre-incubation center of COEP Tech and thus provides mentoring to the students having ideas or innovations and helping them follow steps like ideation, innovation, proof of concept, business model, prototyping, designing. Various innovation and entrepreneurship related activities are conducted as recommended by MoE's (Ministry of Education) Innovation Cell under the guidance of industry experts, alumni, director COEP, patent attorney, and experts from incubators.

CoEP's

Pune Startup Fest 2022

  • The fourth edition of Pune Startup Fest - Pune Startup Fest'22, was presented under the esteemed patronage of ‘Startup India’, ‘Make In India’, ‘Skill India’, ‘MoE's Innovation Cell (Government of India)’ and ‘AGNII’ on 5th and 6th March in hybrid(online+offline) mode. The start-up expo was set up on the Virtual Exhibition Platform‑ExpoSim and there was also an offline exhibition. Pune Start-up Fest'22, with the theme ‑ ‘Caressing Ideas, Cultivating Innovation’, brought together 120 innovative start-ups through a hybrid start-up expo, along with investors, mentors, and C-Level executives from top corporations, industries, and people across the country and successfully epitomized this idea over the two grand days of the fest. PSF'22 was tremendously successful despite being the first ever hybrid (virtual+offline) startup exhibition with overall footfall of 3000+. Over the 4 editions, PSF has witnessed an investment of 5.5 crores.
